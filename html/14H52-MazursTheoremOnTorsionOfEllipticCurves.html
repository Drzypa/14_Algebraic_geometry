Mazur’s theorem on torsion of elliptic curves


Theorem 1 (Mazur).

Let E/Q be an elliptic curve. Then the torsion subgroup Etorsion⁢(Q) is exactly one of the following groups:

ℤ/N⁢ℤ 1≤N≤10 o⁢r N=12
ℤ/2⁢ℤ⊕ℤ/2⁢N⁢ℤ 1≤N≤4

Note: see Nagell-Lutz theorem for an efficient algorithm to compute the torsion subgroup of an elliptic curve defined over ℚ.
